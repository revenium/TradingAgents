<integration_note>
**Wiring point — deviation from ROADMAP locked note (intentional, user-approved 2026-07-03).**
The locked planning note said "wired into research/trader". This plan wires `get_edgehound_decision` into the **market analyst** instead. Rationale: the market analyst runs **unconditionally on every graph run**, so the metered `edgehound_decision` tool event fires deterministically — critical for a live on-stage demo. Researcher/trader tool invocation is probabilistic LLM tool-calling and may not fire on any given run. Success Criterion 1 (3 distinct partner tool events) is satisfied either way; the analyst is the reliable trigger. The gating pattern is identical to `news_analyst.py` (`if cfg.get(...): tools.append(...)`).
</integration_note>
